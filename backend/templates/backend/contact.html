Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us directly!

How long does delivery take?

Our average delivery time is 25-35 minutes within Buea. During peak hours, it might take up to 45 minutes. We'll always keep you updated via WhatsApp.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept mobile money (MTN and Orange), cash on delivery, and you can also contact us via WhatsApp to arrange payment. All methods are secure and convenient.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes! We provide catering services for weddings, corporate events, parties, and other special occasions. Contact us at least 48 hours in advance to discuss your needs.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

You can modify or cancel your order within 5 minutes of placing it by contacting us immediately on WhatsApp. After preparation begins, modifications may not be possible.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

We can accommodate many dietary preferences and restrictions. Please mention any allergies or special requirements when placing your order, and we'll do our best to help.
