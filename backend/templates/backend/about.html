Born from Passion

Caroline Folefack Viviane founded her wholesale business in 2020 with a simple mission: to provide the highest quality ingredients and products to the Buea community, ensuring authentic flavors reach every kitchen.

What began as a small local operation has grown into the region's most trusted wholesale service, serving over 1,500 satisfied customers including restaurants, markets, and individual families with carefully sourced products.

Every product tells a story of quality and reliability, from fresh vegetables and spices to essential household items. Caroline sources her inventory locally, supporting over 50 farmers and suppliers in the Southwest Region.

Her commitment goes beyond just business - she's building a community, supporting local farmers, and creating opportunities for economic growth while bringing convenience and quality to your doorstep.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming Buea's favorite food delivery service.

1

The Beginning

2020

Started as a home kitchen operation with a passionate young entrepreneur, serving traditional Cameroonian dishes to friends and family in Buea.

2

First Orders

2021

Launched our delivery service with WhatsApp ordering. Word spread quickly about our authentic flavors and we gained our first 100 customers.

3

Growth & Recognition

2022-2023

Expanded our menu, hired local staff, and established partnerships with 50+ local suppliers. Became known as Buea's go-to authentic food service.

4

Digital Innovation

2024

Launched our web platform with mobile money integration, making ordering even more convenient for our growing customer base.

5

The Future

2025 & Beyond

Expanding our reach while maintaining quality, introducing catering services, and continuing to serve authentic Cameroonian flavors to even more families.
